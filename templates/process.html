Our Cold-Pressing Process

  1. 1. Seed Sourcing & Cleaning

    We source high-quality seeds from trusted local farmers. Seeds are cleaned to remove dirt, stones and foreign material.

  2. 2. Drying & Conditioning

    Seeds are gently dried and conditioned to the right moisture level — this ensures optimal oil yield without overheating.

  3. 3. Traditional Wood/Stone Pressing

    Seed kernels are pressed in wooden or stone presses at low temperatures. This mechanical pressing extracts oil while preserving nutrients.

  4. 4. Settling & Filtration

    Freshly pressed oil is allowed to settle to remove solids, then gently filtered. We avoid chemical refining — resulting oil is unrefined and nutritious.

  5. 5. Batch Testing & Packaging

    Small-batch testing (physical checks and basic lab tests). Once cleared, oil is stored in dark glass or food-grade bottles to preserve quality.

We maintain low-temperature processing throughout to keep flavor and nutrients intact.
