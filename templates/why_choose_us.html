Why Choose Prakrutik Tattva

Our Story

Prakrutik Tattva began with a simple belief — traditional methods produce healthier, tastier oils. We partner with local farmers, source the best seeds, and press them using wooden/stone press techniques to retain nutrients and natural flavour.

Benefits of Wood-Pressed Oils

  • Nutritive Value: Cold/wood-pressed oils retain more natural antioxidants and vitamins.
  • No Chemicals: We never use solvents, bleaching, or high-heat refining.
  • Rich Flavour: Natural aroma and taste enhance traditional recipes.
  • Sustainable: We support small farmers and use eco-friendly processing.

Quality & Testing

Every batch goes through basic lab testing to ensure purity and free fatty acid (FFA) levels are within safe limits. We label each bottle with harvest and batch information for traceability.
